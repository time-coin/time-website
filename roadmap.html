Phase 7 - Scalability (Post-Mainnet)

Performance, Light Clients & Network Scale

Q3–Q4 2026 — Post-Mainnet

Post-mainnet scaling improvements. These ship as v2.x updates after the April 2026 genesis — mainnet does not require their completion. Scaling the network to handle larger validator sets and higher transaction throughput, and enabling wallets to verify transactions without running a full node.

  • Light client / SPV support (verify txs without a full node)
  • State compression — prune spent UTXOs after sufficient confirmations
  • DHT-based peer discovery (reduce reliance on hardcoded seed nodes)
